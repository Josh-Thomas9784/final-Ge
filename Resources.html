Who We Are

At LeafLink, we're passionate about cleaning the environment. Our journey began the two founders who create LeafLink from the ground up. Since then, we've been dedicated to keep the earth clean and make the world a better place. Through our commitment to fighting for green energy, we strive to change the world for the better.

Join Us

LeafLink is more than just a clean renewable website; we're a community that protects each other and strives for a common goal. Whether you're planting trees or using solar panels, we're here to be with you and cultivate a group that follow the same interests. Join us in our journey to save the planet. Together, we can do anything and everything.
